Services and Amenities

Our gym is equipped with top-of-the-line equipment, including cardio machines, weight lifting machines, and free weights. We also offer a range of fitness classes, including yoga, Pilates, Zumba, and spinning.

Membership Options

At Power Gym, we offer a variety of membership options to suit your needs and budget. Our memberships include full access to our facilities, including our state-of-the-art gym equipment, group fitness classes, and sauna and steam room. Class Schedule

Our gym offers a range of group fitness classes throughout the week, led by certified and experienced instructors. Our classes include early morning yoga and Pilates, mid-day spinning and HIIT classes, and evening Zumba and dance classes. We update our class schedule regularly to ensure that our members have access to the latest and most effective workout routines.
